Service's volunteer opportunites!

One of the more intimidating aspects of entering high school is the volunteering requirement. However, Link Crew is here to help, and show that getting hours is not only easy but can be a great way to help your community, and meet friends!

Below are some volunteer opportunites near American Canyon, that are some great ways that you can get experience and hours, and build connections with the city!

Do you know about a great volunteer opportunity that your classmates might enjoy? Let your link crew leader know, or email our service commisioner at: servicecomissioner@nvusd.org

Title Description Time & Date Contact
Coastal Cleanup Get volunteer hours with friends by cleaning up the city! June 8th, 2020 For more info: call Parks and Rec at (707) 648-7275.
Campus Beautification Link Crew invites you to Campus Beautification! Get hours and help keep your campus clean! Every Wednesday, from after school to 4:30! contact Link Crew at @achslc, or ask any Link Crew Leader!
Spring Cleaning Earn hours helping the P.E. department clean out storage! Snacks will be provided for volunteers. July 1st, 2020 e-mail Mr. Smith at smith@nvusd.org!
July 4th Parade Looking for parade keepers, or game organizers for AC's 8th Fourth of July parade! July 4th, 2020 For more info: call Parks and Rec at (707) 648-7275.
